EyeCandy DB

This is a bitstarter project to create an updated online presence for Portland, Oregon's Eye Candy VJs.

Portland’s Eye Candy VJs serve up classic and contemporary music videos in various watering holes four days a week. But finding the video you want to request is often way harder than it ought to be. Paper books with lists of videos sorted by artist? Fuck that! We're hell-bent on creating a better experience whenever you go to Eye Candy nights around town.

Imagine a world where you and your friends don't need to huddle around a physical book in a dimly lit bar. What if you could search a database that was constantly updated with your phone? What if you could request videos without leaving your seat? Don't have cash? Don't worry about it! Connect EyeCandyDB to your bank account and tip right from your phone.
